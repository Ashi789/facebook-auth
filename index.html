User Data Deletion Instructions

In compliance with Facebook's policy, if you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the steps below.

How to Remove Your Data:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Find and select our app from the list of connected apps.
  3. Click on **Remove** to delete the app and associated data.

Request Manual Data Deletion:

If you have signed up directly with our app and wish to delete your account and associated data, please email us at support@yourapp.com with your request.

For further assistance, contact us at support@yourapp.com.
